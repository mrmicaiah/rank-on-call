## Why this doc exists

The **lane split** (see `PROJECT_MASTER.md`) requires every Rank On Call *diagnostic* article to cross-link to the relevant Sites On Call *teaching* article. SOC has a mature library — ~22 articles as of July 2026 — already ranking across much of Rank On Call's target keyword pool.

This doc is the canonical list, used for two jobs:

1. **Cross-link targets** — every Rank On Call article hands off to the SOC article that teaches the fix.
2. **Collision check** — every new Rank On Call topic gets checked against this list first, so the two sites reinforce each other instead of competing for the same searches and splitting their own authority.

> **Source: sitesoncall.com/articles, captured 2026-07-21 — a point-in-time snapshot. SOC publishes regularly; re-check the live articles page before treating this as complete.**

### 🏆 FLAGSHIP BRIDGE

**Most North Alabama Contractors Are Losing Google to Middlemen** — a market-level **data study**. SOC ran the numbers across a whole region and published what it found.

Rank On Call's entire pitch is the personal version of exactly that: **"we run that study on YOU, personally, for $39."**

That makes it the single strongest thematic bridge in the library. **Every visibility article can reference it**, and it works in both directions — SOC's study proves the method is real and already run at scale; Rank On Call makes it personal.

Now that all 22 are listed, **a later pass could upgrade some cross-links to better matches.** Two articles in particular weren't available to the original matching and may fit some **Leads hub** articles better than what's currently assigned:

**Not urgent** — the existing cross-links are all valid, just not necessarily optimal. Flagged as a known refinement, not a defect.

Also note the vertical-specific articles (landscaping ×3, plumber ×2, HVAC, roofing, electrician). Rank On Call is **contractors-only, no sub-vertical split** for now — a settled decision in `PROJECT_MASTER.md` — so these are cross-link targets, **not** a template to mirror. Do not create a Rank On Call article per trade.
